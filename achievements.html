Achievements

Launching 104 satellites through one rocket:

-In our 39th mission, the PSLV-C37 successfully carried and deployed a record 104 satellites in sun-synchronous orbits on 15 February 2017.

South Asia Satellite (GSAT-9) launch:

-On 5th May 2017 ,we successfully launched the GSAT-9 for the South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation (SAARC) region.

Maiden Lunar Mission:

-Chandrayaan discovered water on the moon back in 2009
